Website Features

You can change the color theme for the website from the 'Set Color' and 'Toggle Dark' buttons at the top of this page.

Your settings will be saved for the next time you visit this site.

AmazonSMP Sections

The AmazonSMP page has toggleable sections, you can show and hide content by clicking on headers (some some tables can also be toggled by clicking on the header (first one or two rows)).

Updates

General updates will be listed on this page in the updates section. You can click on the boxes to expand them and show update details.

Soon website updates will also be listed on there.

Report / Request

To report an issue or suggestion for the website: create a new issue on the gitlab project.

Contribution

Only AmazonSMP Members will be allowed to contribute.

If you would like to contribute to the website, please contact Dohst.

After your first contribution your name will be listed in the 'Website' section of 'Credit'.
